2 points per minute of movement
What is "intentional" movement?
  • Many of us have Fitbits or Apple products that count our steps or time moving, this is NOT a step challenge.
  • Examples of intentional movement: a 45-minute walk, a 20 minute jog, a bike ride, or swimming laps.
  • Counting your active time while playing a sport is also pretty tricky. I'll leave it up to you to figure that out.
  • Challenge is not limited to just walking, jogging, biking, or swimming.

How do I participate?

It's easy. Log your activity as often as you like.
