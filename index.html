1 point per second of planking
Scoring guide for squats
  • Regular planks:
    1 second = 1 point
  • Beginner planks from knees:
    1 second = .5 points
  • Beginners: take your # of seconds and divide by 2
  • For example, 90 seconds of beginner = 45 seconds regular. Log 45, not 90.

How do I participate?

It's easy. Log your activity as often as you like.
